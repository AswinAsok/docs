MakeMyPass is a smart event management and ticketing platform designed to streamline guest handling, check-ins, and payments. Whether you're hosting small meetups or large-scale conferences, MakeMyPass simplifies event registration, enhances attendee engagement, and ensures a seamless experience for organizers and participants alike.

## Getting Started

Setting up your event on MakeMyPass is quick and straightforward:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up on MakeMyPass and set up your organizer profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Event">
    Define event details, ticket types, and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Selling Tickets">
    Share your event link and start accepting registrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Effortlessly">
    Track attendees, handle payments, and generate reports.
