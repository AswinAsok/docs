## Adding a New Sponsor

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/event/eventPage/sponsers/sponserAddModal.png" className="rounded-lg shadow-md" />
</Frame>

1. Click the **Update Sponsors** button on the event page.
2. In the modal that opens, click the **Add Sponsor** button in the top-right corner.
3. In the new modal, enter the following details:
   - **Sponsor Name**
   - **Create or Select a Sponsor Tier** (e.g., `Gold`, `Silver`, `Bronze`)
   - **Upload an Image** for the sponsor logo

> **Note:** All sponsor details will be displayed on the **Registration Page**.

## Changing Sponsor Order

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/event/eventPage/sponsers/updateTier.png" className="rounded-lg shadow-md" />
</Frame>

- Once all sponsors have been added, you can **rearrange** their order by dragging them within the **Add Sponsors** modal by using the <Icon icon="up-down-left-right" iconType="solid"/> against each tier name.
- Click **Yes** against the alert to confirm the new order.
- The sponsor list will be displayed in the same order **after the registration form** on the event page.

## Editing or Deleting an Existing Sponsor

- You can **edit** or **delete** a sponsor by using the respective **Edit** and **Delete** icons next to each sponsor in the listing section.
