---
title: "Event Logs"
description: "The Event Logs section provides a comprehensive overview of all emails and WhatsApp messages sent to participants, along with their statuses."
---

### Mail Logs
- This section contains logs of all emails sent to participants, regardless of category.
- Clicking on a particular log allows viewing of the **subject** and **body** of that email.
- If the recipient has opened the email, the **open time** will be displayed alongside the following details:
  - Time the email was sent
  - Recipient's email address

### WhatsApp Logs
- Similar to **Mail Logs**, this section displays logs of messages sent via WhatsApp.
- To enable viewing, toggle the **WhatsApp Logs** button.
- The logs display:
  - Time the message was sent
  - Phone number of the recipient
