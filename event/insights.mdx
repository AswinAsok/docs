### Custom Insights

Organizers can create custom charts (bar, pie, line, or table) using form data to track additional metrics relevant to their event.

#### Creating Custom Insights

Follow these steps to create a custom insight for your event:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Create New Insights">
    Click the **"Create New Insights"** tab at the bottom of the Insights page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Insight">
    In the modal that appears, select the form field you want to analyze and choose your preferred graph type (bar, pie, line, or table).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Insight">
    Click the **"Create Insights"** button to generate and add your new custom insight to the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

> **Note:** To delete a custom insight, locate it on your dashboard and click the **"Delete"** button at the top right corner of the insight box.

### Public Insights Sharing

Easily share event analytics with others, even if they don’t have a MakeMyPass account.

#### Publishing the Insights Page

Follow these steps to make your event insights publicly accessible:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Share Button">
    Locate and click the **"Share"** button at the top right corner of the Insights page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Public Access">
    A modal will appear. In this modal, click the **"Pubblish"** option to enable public sharing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the Public Link">
    Once enabled, the **public link** to the Insights page will be displayed. Copy this link to share it with others.
  </Step>
</Steps>

> If you wish to revoke public access, open the same modal and click **"Unpublish"** to disable the public link.
