## Page View Analytics

Track your event page performance with comprehensive visitor data and conversion metrics.

### Timeline Analytics

View detailed line graphs showing page view statistics from the day your event page launched until today. This visualization helps you:

- Identify traffic patterns over time
- Spot peak visitor periods
- Correlate marketing efforts with traffic increases
- Track conversion rate trends

### Metadata Analytics

Gain deeper insights about your visitors with detailed breakdowns across multiple dimensions. Each table includes:
- **Page Views**: Total number of visits
- **Registration Count**: How many visitors registered
- **Conversion Rate %**: Percentage of visitors who completed registration

### Using Page View Analytics Effectively

- **Marketing Optimization**: Focus resources on browsers, devices, and regions with higher conversion rates
- **Technical Improvements**: Ensure your event page works well on the most common browsers and devices
- **Geographic Targeting**: Adjust marketing efforts based on regional performance
- **Content Localization**: Consider adding language options based on locale analytics

> **Tip**: Compare conversion rates across different dimensions to identify opportunities for improvement. For example, if mobile conversion rates are lower than desktop, your page might need mobile optimization.
